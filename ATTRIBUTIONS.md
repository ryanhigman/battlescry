# BattleScry ... Token Art Credits

a library of free game icons. All 139 icons used here are licensed under the
[Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 Unported (CC BY 3.0)](https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/3.0/) license.

**Modifications:** the original SVG icons were exported to PNG and are hosted at
`battlescry.com/tokens/` for use as circular battlemap tokens. The artwork itself is unchanged.
